Our Achievements

Jiva Kavach – Patented Innovation for Cattle Safety, Successfully Deployed On-Field

India records over 25,000 incidents annually involving stray cattle on railway tracks, leading to significant loss of livestock, damage to railway infrastructure, and disruption of train services. These accidents not only cause emotional and financial distress to farmers but also result in delays, repairs, and economic losses for Indian Railways. To address this critical issue, we developed Jiva Kavach—a first-of-its-kind preventive safety system for cattle. Successfully deployed in field trials, Jiva Kavach has shown proven effectiveness in deterring livestock from straying onto railway tracks, thereby reducing the risk of accidents. This socially impactful solution has been awarded a patent, recognizing its originality and public value. This achievement stands as a testament to our commitment to rural safety, animal welfare, and infrastructure protection—offering a scalable and sustainable solution to a problem that affects thousands of lives and livelihoods every year.

Next-Gen Anti-Counterfeiting for Smarter Returns - Patented

We're excited to introduce a cutting-edge anti-counterfeiting solution that enhances product authenticity, security, and consumer trust. Built to combat return fraud, product swapping, and counterfeiting, it offers a tamper-evident, one-time-use verification system that keeps products traceable and unaltered throughout their lifecycle. This discreet technology detects fraudulent returns—like wardrobing or counterfeit swaps—instantly, helping businesses avoid financial and reputational losses. It integrates seamlessly without affecting product design or sustainability, and it's effective across industries including fashion, retail, logistics, and high-value goods. By making authenticity checks void after use, this innovation provides a reliable safeguard for brands and holds promise for secure applications like packaging. It’s not just fraud protection—it’s a smarter, more secure way forward.
