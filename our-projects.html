Our Achievements

Innovation Award for Animal Safety Technology

Wallenc Pvt. Ltd. was honored with the 2024 National Innovation Award for our pioneering device that uses non-lethal deterrents to protect livestock from railway accidents. This project marked a turning point in tech-for-good innovation.

Partnership with Indian Railways

We partnered with Indian Railways to test and deploy our AI-based cattle detection and deterrent system, greatly improving trackside safety and reducing train delays.

Tech Expo Showcase 2025

Wallenc showcased our latest encryption hardware at the International Tech Expo 2025. Our booth featured live demos of secure communication devices using AES-256 encryption — earning us strong investor interest.
